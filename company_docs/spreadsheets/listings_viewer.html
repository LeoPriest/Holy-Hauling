How to Use This App

1

Browse — scroll down to see all items. Each card shows the photo, name, price, and status.

2

Tap any card to see a bigger photo and full details.

3

Search by typing a word in the bar at the top — e.g. "dresser" or "mattress".

4

Filter by status — swipe the colored buttons left or right, then tap one.

5

Sort using the dropdown below the filters.
